# Product Direction

## Positioning

PRO X should feel closer to:

- private banking software
- a luxury automotive launch
- an invitation-only investment club

It should feel less like:

- a classifieds portal
- a spreadsheet turned into cards
- a crowded proptech dashboard

## Content framing

- Show a tiny, highly curated selection.
- Lead with why the opportunity matters, not a wall of metadata.
- Treat the detail panel as a mini digital pitch deck.
- Keep access tiers visible because exclusivity is part of the concept.

## Visual cues

- Use dark surfaces with warm metallic emphasis.
- Let the map and atmosphere do a lot of the emotional work.
- Use stronger headline typography than body typography.
- Favor spacing and contrast over adding more widgets.
